Instructions

In the game you should imagine that you want to know more about a topic that is mentioned on top of the page.

Your task is to select 10 relevant documents about the topic in as few clicks as possible.

Below the description of the topic, a list of different documents will be shown. You can click on the documents to indicate that you would use that document to gather more information about the topic. If you select a relevant document, it will be highlighted in green and a will appear on the right of the title. If the document you selected is not relevant, it will be highlighted in red and a will appear on the right.

On the left of the page, you can filter the documents based on the categories they belong to. On the right of the page, you can see how many clicks you have left (starting with 50 clicks) and how many relevant documents you still need to select. The more clicks you have left after selecting 10 relevant documents, the higher your score! You can check your score at Highscores.
