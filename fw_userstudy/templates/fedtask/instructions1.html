Instructions

In this game you collect documents about the keyword query at the top of the page.

Your task is to click on 10 documents relevant to the query in as few clicks as possible.

If it is relevant it a will appear. If it is not relevant a will appear.

What makes a document relevant? Well, you have to find out. To help you, before each round, we provide two example documents, one relevant and one irrelevant, and a description of why. If you are ready to go press Let's get started. During the game the description is available through this button: Show explanations and examples of relevant/irrelevant results.
A tip: You may see duplicated results and these should be judged independently.

On the right of the page, you can see how many clicks you have left (starting with 50 clicks) and how many relevant documents you still need to select. The more clicks you have left after selecting 10 relevant documents, the higher your score! You can check your score at Highscores.

In case you feel a query is especially difficult for you, you can decide to given up after 25 clicks. A I give up! button will show up then.

Note: if you give up, you will not receive any points for that query!
