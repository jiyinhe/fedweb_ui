Instructions

In this game you collect documents about the keyword query at the top of the page.

Your task is to select 10 documents relevant to the query in as few clicks as possible.

The persons who have posted these queries have already marked the documents they found relevant - your job is to locate these documents as quickly as possible.

Select a document by clicking on it. If it is relevant it will be highlighted in green and a will appear. If it is not relevant, it will be highlighted in red and a will appear.

On the left of the page, you can filter the documents based on the categories they belong to.

On the right of the page, you can see how many clicks you have left (starting with 50 clicks) and how many relevant documents you still need to select. The more clicks you have left after selecting 10 relevant documents, the higher your score! You can check your score at Highscores.

In case you feel a query is especially difficult for you, you can decide to given up after 25 clicks. A I give up! button will show up then.

Note: if you give up, you will not receive any points for that query!

What makes a document relevant? Well, you have to find out. on the top part of the page, you can see two example documents, one is relevant, and the other is irrelevant. A tip: You may see duplicates and these should be judged independently.
